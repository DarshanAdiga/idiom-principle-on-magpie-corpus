 # Stockton Unified, Stockton Kings asking for donations of school supplies for Stuff the Bus event
 The Record
 Stockton Unified School District in partnership with the Stockton Kings will hold a drive-thru stuff the bus event.
 Donations are needed of the following items: backpacks, binders, headphones, USB flash drives, pens and pencils. Items will be collected in a drive-thru from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. Saturday at the Rosemarie Lane parking lot at Stagg High School.
