 # Small earthquake recorded beneath University of Tennessee at Knoxville. Did you feel it?
 Brittany Crocker
 Knoxville News Sentinel
 View Comments
 A small earthquake was recorded under the University of Tennessee at Knoxville campus Tuesday night, according to the United States Geological Survey.
 The magnitude 2.3 earthquake occurred at about 10:20 p.m. at a depth of 4.4 miles near Circle Drive.
 Did you feel it? If so, let the Geological Survey know at earthquake.usgs.gov.
