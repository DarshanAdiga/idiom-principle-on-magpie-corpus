 # Driver pulled from SUV moments before crash with train in Oxnard
 Joe Curley
 Ventura County Star
 View Comments
 The driver of an SUV broken down on railroad tracks in Oxnard was pulled out of the vehicle by another motorist moments before it was struck by a train Sunday night, authorities said.
 The accident took place at 8:58 p.m. at the intersection of Oxnard Boulevard and Gonzales Road, said Sgt. Chris Inglehart of the Oxnard Police Department.
 The driver had been in an crash with another car and stopped on the tracks. The driver was calling 911 when a northbound Amtrak train approached, Inglehart said.
 Another motorist recognized what was happening and intervened.
 “Somebody got out of a car,” Inglehart said. “I don’t know if it was a driver or a passenger.”
 That person pulled the SUV driver to safety.
 No injuries were reported.
