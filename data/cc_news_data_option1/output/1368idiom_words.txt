While packed parks have had a lot of coverage, with police dispersing crowds of sunbathers, one thing we haven't heard so much about are the measures being taken at Tube stations.
 British Transport Police are enforcing the rules at stations across London.
 One person tweeted to say they had seen police stopping people at Canning Town station, asking for ID and why they were out.
 If the reason wasn't classed as essential work they were barred from entering the station.
