Harris's record dates back nearly 30 years, when he was convicted for selling cocaine in 1991. He was later convicted of simple robbery in 1993, and once again in 1994, and on a charge of theft under US$500 in 2005.
 Prosecutors agreed to his release after he was granted a new hearing in July by the Louisiana Supreme Court.
