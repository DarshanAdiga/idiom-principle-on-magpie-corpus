Klingner adds two to Galesburg team
 GALESBURG -- Klingner & Associates P.C. has welcomed Melissa Goodwin to its architectural department as an architectural designer. Goodwin, who is based in Klingner's Galesburg office, will provide technical support for architectural projects designed in Illinois, Missouri and Iowa.
 A graduate of Iowa State University, Goodwin holds a master's in architecture and a master's in community and regional planning. Her experience includes MEP, architectural and structural drafting and field verification; underground utility locates; and administrative documentation for power plant facilities.
 Klingner has also added Erika Hostens to its architectural department as an interior designer. She is based in the Galesburg office and will provide technical support for architectural projects designed in Illinois, Missouri and Iowa.
 A graduate of University of Northern Iowa, Hostens holds a bachelor's degree in interior design with an emphasis in business and a minor in marketing. Her experience includes interior design, space planning, project management, commercial furniture design and specifications, and commercial consulting, as well as sales and marketing experience.
