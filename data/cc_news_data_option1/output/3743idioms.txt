 # Minerva falls to Beaver Local 56-7
 Rob Todor
 The Alliance Review
 LISBON — Beaver Local scored four first-quarter touchdowns and went on to defeat Minerva 56-7 in a non-conference football game on Friday night.
 Dylan Moore returned the opening kickoff 84 yards for a touchdown and Caleb White added a 62-yard kickoff return for a score following a safety. Zane Kinsey and Tanner Johnston also found the end zone as the Beavers led 29-0.
 Minerva's lone score was a 4-yard run by Brayden Costea with 7:15 remaining in the fourth quarter.
 Ryder Hartshorn led the Lions with 61 yards rushing on 17 carries. Jimmy Common added 47 yards on 12 attempts. Costea completed three of five passes for 43 yards.
 Johnston rushed for 66 yards and completed five of eight passes for 90 yards and two scores for the Beavers.

 # Mount Union-St. John Fisher game on Raider Rewind
 The Alliance Review
 Another classic Mount Union football game is scheduled to be broadcast by WIVM-TV in Canton and simulcast online Saturday, September 19.
 At 2 p.m., the station will broadcast a 2007 NCAA Playoff national quarterfinal game between Mount Union and St. John Fisher. The game featured future NFL players Pierre Garcon and Cecil Shorts III along with current Mount Union coaches Geoff Dartt, Chris Kappas and Daryl Ely.
 WIVM-TV is on channel 39 over the air, channel 989 on Spectrum cable in Stark County and online at WIVMTV.com. Fans can also add the LiveStream app on their smart TV and search for WIVM.
 Joe Tait, Jeff Shreve and Harry Paidas have called the action on air for games produced by Image Video, which operates WIVM-TV.
 Image Video of North Canton produced all the games and operates WIVM-TV.
